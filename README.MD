# 🎯 Why HireMatch?

> Modern hiring starts long before a recruiter reads your resume.

Today, **Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)** automatically filter resumes before they ever reach a human recruiter.

HireMatch helps candidates bridge that gap by combining **AI-powered resume analysis**, **ATS compatibility scoring**, and **actionable recommendations** into one seamless platform.

Instead of simply displaying a score, HireMatch explains:

- ✅ Why your ATS score is low
- ✅ Which skills are missing
- ✅ Which sections need improvement
- ✅ How to rewrite weak resume bullets
- ✅ How to optimize your resume for recruiters
